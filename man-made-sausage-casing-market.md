<p><p>The man-made sausage casing market consists of various types, each suited for different applications. Collagen casings utilize animal collagen, offering a natural feel and durability, ideal for fresh and cooked sausages. Cellulose casings are plant-based, often used for skinless sausages; they are removed before consumption. Plastic casings provide a moisture barrier and are designed for long shelf life, commonly used in cold cuts. Other types include fibrous casings, catering to specialty products, enhancing texture and presentation.</p></p>

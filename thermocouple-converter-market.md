<p><p>The thermocouple converter market is primarily categorized into two types: signal converters and digital converters. Signal converters process the analog signals generated by thermocouples, amplifying and converting them into a usable format for further analysis or control. In contrast, digital converters directly transform thermocouple signals into digital formats, enabling easier integration with digital systems and more precise readings. Both types play a crucial role in industrial applications, ensuring accurate temperature measurement and control for various processes.</p></p>

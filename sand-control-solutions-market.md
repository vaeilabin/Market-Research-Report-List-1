<p><strong>The Sand Control Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sand Screens</li><li>Gravel Pack</li><li>Frac Pack</li><li>Inflow Control Devices</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sand control solutions market encompasses various methods to prevent sand production during hydrocarbon extraction. Sand screens are filters that separate sand from produced fluids. Gravel packs involve placing gravel around the wellbore to stabilize formations. Frac packs are a combination of fracturing and gravel packing, enhancing flow efficiency. Inflow control devices regulate fluid entry and improve reservoir management. Other market solutions include chemical treatments and mechanical barriers, all aimed at optimizing production while minimizing sand-related issues.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Wrist Supports Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wrist Supports With Thumb</li><li>Wrist Supports Without Thumb</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wrist supports market can be categorized into two main types: wrist supports with thumb and wrist supports without thumb. Wrist supports with thumb provide additional stabilization and protection for both the wrist and thumb joint, making them ideal for conditions like de Quervain's tenosynovitis or thumb sprains. In contrast, wrist supports without thumb focus solely on wrist support and are suitable for general wrist injuries or conditions such as carpal tunnel syndrome, allowing for a wider range of hand movement.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Mechanical/Scanning Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive 3D Light Detection and Ranging (LiDAR) market is primarily divided into two types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR employs fixed sensors without moving parts, providing compact, durable solutions ideal for automotive applications. Conversely, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR utilizes rotating parts to capture a wider field of view, offering high-resolution data but often at a higher cost and complexity. Both technologies are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety and enabling autonomous driving capabilities.</p></p>

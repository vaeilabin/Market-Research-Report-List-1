<p><strong>The High Temperature Recovery Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-temperature recovery wheels market comprises three main types: condensation rotors, hygroscopic rotors, and sorption rotors. Condensation rotors are designed to recover heat by changing the state of moisture in the air, making them effective in humid environments. Hygroscopic rotors utilize materials that absorb moisture, resulting in enhanced thermal performance and energy efficiency. Sorption rotors employ chemical processes to capture and release moisture while recovering heat, optimizing energy use in HVAC systems, particularly in industrial applications.</p></p>

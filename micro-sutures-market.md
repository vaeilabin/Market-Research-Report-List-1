<p><p>The micro sutures market is divided into nylon and polypropylene types, each with unique characteristics. Conversely, polypropylene sutures are non-absorbable and offer superior biocompatibility, making them ideal for long-term tissue support. Both types are widely used in surgeries such as ophthalmology, plastic surgery, and wound closures, catering to the specific needs of different medical applications and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>

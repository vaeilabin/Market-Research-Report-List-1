<p><strong>The Hand held Veterinary Refractometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Refractometers</li><li>Digital Refractometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld veterinary refractometer market is divided into two main types: optical refractometers and digital refractometers. Optical refractometers use light and prisms to determine the refractive index of a fluid, allowing veterinarians to assess the concentration of substances like protein or glucose in animal fluids. Digital refractometers, on the other hand, provide electronic readings and often feature advanced data processing capabilities, offering increased accuracy and ease of use. Both types are essential tools for veterinary diagnostics and care.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balanced Coil Systems</li><li>Ferrous-in-Foil Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical metal detector market features two primary types: balanced coil systems and ferrous-in-foil systems. Balanced coil systems utilize dual coils to create a balanced electromagnetic field, capable of detecting non-ferrous metals, such as aluminum and copper, alongside ferrous metals. Ferrous-in-foil systems, meanwhile, are specifically designed to identify ferrous contaminants nestled within foil packaging, ensuring product integrity and safety. Both systems are vital in maintaining quality control in pharmaceutical manufacturing, protecting consumers from contamination.</p></p>

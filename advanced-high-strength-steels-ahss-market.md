<p><p>Advanced High-Strength Steels (AHSS) are categorized into three main types based on alloy composition: Low Alloy, Medium Alloy, and High Alloy. Low Alloy AHSS typically contains small amounts of alloying elements to enhance strength and ductility, making it suitable for automotive applications. Medium Alloy AHSS offers improved mechanical properties through moderate alloying, balancing strength and weight. High Alloy AHSS features higher quantities of alloying elements, delivering exceptional strength and performance, ideal for demanding structural applications in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>The cellulosic polymers market encompasses three primary types: cellulose esters, cellulose ethers, and regenerated cellulose. Cellulose esters are derived from the acetylation or propionylation of cellulose, widely used in coatings and plastics. Cellulose ethers, modified cellulose compounds, serve as thickening, binding, and film-forming agents in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. Regenerated cellulose, produced through dissolving cellulose and re-forming it, finds applications in textiles and packaging, known for its biodegradability and versatile properties. Together, these types drive innovation in various industries.</p></p>

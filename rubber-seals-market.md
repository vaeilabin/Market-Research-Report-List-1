O-rings are circular seals used to prevent leakage in various mechanical applications, providing a reliable barrier against fluids and gases. Gaskets serve as flat seals that fill space between two surfaces, ensuring airtight and watertight seals in engines and pipelines. The "others" category includes specialized seals like rubber adhesives and customized solutions, catering to specific industry needs and varying applications across sectors such as automotive and manufacturing.</p></p>

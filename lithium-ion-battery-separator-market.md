<p><strong>The Lithium-Ion Battery Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Layer (PP/PE)</li><li>Bilayer (PP/PE, PP/PP)</li><li>Trilayer (PP/PE/PP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium-ion battery separator market features three main types: Single-Layer, Bilayer, and Trilayer separators. Single-Layer separators, typically made from polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE), offer basic performance. Bilayer separators combine two materials, such as PP/PE or PP/PP, enhancing thermal stability and safety. Trilayer separators, with a configuration of PP/PE/PP, provide superior mechanical strength and electrolyte retention, significantly improving the battery's overall efficiency and lifespan. Each type targets specific performance needs in battery applications.</p></p>

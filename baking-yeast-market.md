<p><ul><li>Regular Active Dry Yeast</li><li>Instant Yeast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baking yeast market primarily includes two types: regular active dry yeast and instant yeast. Regular active dry yeast requires activation in warm water before use, making it suitable for traditional baking methods. Instant yeast, on the other hand, can be mixed directly with dry ingredients without prior activation, offering convenience and faster rising times. Both types cater to various consumer preferences and baking applications, from home bakers to commercial producers, enhancing the versatility and efficiency of baking processes.</p></p>

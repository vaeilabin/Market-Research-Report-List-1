<p><strong>The Defoamer for Injection Molding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eliminate Bubbles</li><li>Suppress Bubbles</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the injection molding market, defoamers are categorized primarily into three types. "Eliminate Bubbles" defoamers actively break down and remove air bubbles during the molding process, ensuring a smooth finish. "Suppress Bubbles" defoamers work to reduce bubble formation by modifying the surface tension, thus preventing air entrapment. "Others" encompasses various specialized defoamers that may target specific materials or processes, offering tailored solutions to enhance the overall quality and efficiency of molded products.</p></p>

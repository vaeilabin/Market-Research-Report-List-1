<p><p>The medicine bottle market is categorized into three primary types: plastic, glass, and metal. Plastic bottles are lightweight, shatter-resistant, and often cost-effective, making them popular for many pharmaceuticals. Glass containers offer excellent chemical resistance and preserve the integrity of sensitive medications, but they are heavier and more fragile. Metal bottles are durable and provide an airtight seal, making them suitable for specific products, although they are less common. Each material serves distinct needs based on durability, safety, and product compatibility.</p></p>

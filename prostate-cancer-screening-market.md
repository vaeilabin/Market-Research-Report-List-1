<p><p>Prostate cancer screening encompasses various methods to detect the disease early. Physical exams and digital rectal exams evaluate prostate abnormalities. The Gleason score grades cancer aggressiveness, while the TNM score assesses tumor size and spread. Genetic tests analyze hereditary risks, and biopsies provide definitive diagnosis by examining tissue samples. Together, these approaches enhance early detection and management of prostate cancer.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>TSH Test</li><li>FT3 Test</li><li>FT4 Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thyroid functioning tests market comprises three key tests: TSH (Thyroid Stimulating Hormone) test, FT3 (Free Triiodothyronine) test, and FT4 (Free Thyroxine) test. The TSH test assesses the pituitary gland's stimulation of the thyroid, indicating overall thyroid health. The FT3 test measures the active form of triiodothyronine, while the FT4 test evaluates the primary hormone produced by the thyroid. Together, these tests help diagnose thyroid disorders, monitor treatment effectiveness, and guide clinical decisions, making them crucial in endocrine healthcare.</p></p>

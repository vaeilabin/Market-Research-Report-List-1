<p><p>Renal denervation catheters are specialized medical devices used in a minimally invasive procedure aimed at treating resistant hypertension by disrupting the nerve pathways around the renal arteries. This technique targets the sympathetic nervous system, which plays a crucial role in blood pressure regulation. The catheters deliver energy, typically in the form of radiofrequency or ultrasound, to ablate renal nerves, potentially leading to significant reductions in blood pressure.</p><p>The Renal Denervation Catheters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.9% during the forecast period.

<p><p>High efficiency heterojunction solar cells (HJT) are advanced photovoltaic technologies that utilize a combination of crystalline silicon and thin film layers for enhanced performance. The market is segmented into monofacial and bifacial cells. Monofacial cells capture sunlight from one side, offering reliable efficiency for traditional applications. In contrast, bifacial cells harness sunlight from both sides, delivering increased energy output by utilizing reflected light. Bifacial cells are gaining traction due to their ability to maximize energy harvest in diverse environments.</p></p>
